About Prophet Henry Attah

Prophet Henry Attah is a prophet, father, teacher, and wise master builder with a strong prophetic and apostolic emphasis. He leads with a clear divine charge: "Build Me a company of Burning, Balanced Men who will seek God's heart."

As President and Chief Steward of Fire Clan Global Ministries, his assignment is to raise sons shaped by intimacy, established in wisdom, and prepared for influence. His ministry advances awakening, priesthood, leadership, and alignment with the heart of God.

Through Mentorship and Ramah Academy, he equips believers and leaders with spiritual depth and practical direction for life and calling.

The people he is raising will be voices in nations and across spheres — men who can speak for God at any given time.
